Gargamel Dota League

Community-Run Inhouse League

What is Gargamel?

Gargamel is an inhouse Dota 2 league run entirely by its community. Unlike public matchmaking, every game in Gargamel is organized through our Discord server where a custom bot handles matchmaking, team balancing, and lobby creation automatically.

How It Works

Players register through our website by linking their Discord and Steam accounts. Once registered, you can queue up for matches directly in Discord. When enough players are ready, the bot automatically balances teams based on player ratings and creates a Dota 2 lobby for the match.

Key Features

  • + Automated matchmaking and team balancing based on player skill ratings
  • + Auto-created Dota 2 lobbies — no manual lobby setup needed
  • + Discord-based coordination — queue, communicate, and play all from one place
  • + Community-driven moderation and league management
  • + Rating system that adjusts over time to reflect your true skill level

Why Inhouse?

Inhouse leagues provide a more controlled and competitive environment compared to public matchmaking. Games are played exclusively among registered community members, leading to better communication, fewer abandons, and more balanced matches. It's Dota the way it's meant to be played.
